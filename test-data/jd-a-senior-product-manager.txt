Helix Systems — Senior Product Manager, Platform

About Helix Systems
Helix Systems is a rapidly growing Series C enterprise SaaS company headquartered in San Francisco. We build AI-powered operational intelligence software used by more than 600 enterprise clients across financial services, insurance, and healthcare. Our platform processes over 4 billion data events per month and our team of 320 people is on a mission to make complex business operations effortlessly intelligent.

We recently closed a 95 million dollar Series C and are expanding our product organisation to support our next phase of growth.

The Role
We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to own the strategy and execution for Helix's core platform — the shared infrastructure layer that underpins all of our product lines. This is a high-impact, high-visibility role that will work closely with our CTO, VP of Engineering, and the heads of our three vertical product lines.

Principal Duties
- Define and own the multi-year product vision and roadmap for the Helix platform, balancing technical infrastructure investment with customer-facing capabilities
- Lead a cross-functional squad of 10 to 15 engineers, designers, and data scientists through quarterly planning cycles and weekly execution sprints
- Drive alignment on roadmap priorities across three internal product teams and senior leadership, using structured frameworks such as RICE and OKR-based goal setting
- Own the platform's key product metrics: time-to-value for new enterprise clients, platform reliability, API adoption, and developer experience scores
- Lead customer discovery with enterprise accounts, including executive business reviews and joint roadmap sessions with strategic customers
- Partner with Sales, Customer Success, and Solutions Engineering on complex enterprise deals where platform capabilities are a key evaluation criterion
- Define and ship self-serve and API-first capabilities that enable Helix's partner ecosystem to build on top of the platform

Required Qualifications
- 7 or more years of product management experience, with at least 4 years in a senior or lead PM role at a B2B SaaS company
- Demonstrated experience owning a platform or infrastructure product surface
- Strong track record of delivering complex, multi-stakeholder roadmaps on time
- Fluency with data-driven prioritisation frameworks (RICE, MoSCoW, OKR alignment)
- Experience leading cross-functional teams of 10 or more through full product development cycles
- Excellent executive communication skills — able to present confidently to C-level stakeholders and enterprise clients
- Deep understanding of enterprise sales cycles and how product decisions affect win rates and expansion revenue

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience at a company that has scaled from Series B to Series D or beyond
- Familiarity with API product development and developer experience (DX) best practices
- Experience with AI or ML-powered product features
- Exposure to financial services, insurance, or healthcare verticals
- MBA or equivalent advanced degree

What We Offer
- Competitive base salary: 180,000 to 220,000 dollars plus equity
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision benefits
- 4,000 dollar annual learning and development budget
- Flexible hybrid work from our San Francisco HQ
- 20 days PTO plus 10 company holidays
