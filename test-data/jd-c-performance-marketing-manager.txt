Aura Wellness — Performance Marketing Manager

About Aura Wellness
Aura Wellness is a fast-growing DTC wellness brand best known for our flagship sleep and recovery tracker, worn by more than 1.2 million customers globally. We are a Series C company of 230 people with headquarters in New York City and a product development team in Helsinki. Our mission is to make personalised health insights accessible to everyone.

We are growing our performance marketing team as we prepare to launch into two new product categories and expand into three new international markets over the next 18 months.

The Role
We are looking for a Performance Marketing Manager to own paid media strategy and execution across our core digital channels: Meta, Google, TikTok, and Pinterest. This role reports to the VP of Growth and will collaborate closely with creative, lifecycle, and analytics teams.

Principal Duties
- Own paid media strategy, execution, and performance across Meta Ads, Google Ads (Search, Shopping, Performance Max), TikTok for Business, and Pinterest Ads
- Manage a monthly media budget of 1.5 to 2.5 million dollars across all channels, optimising for customer acquisition cost and return on ad spend targets
- Lead creative testing strategy: brief, evaluate, and iterate on static, video, and UGC creative assets in partnership with the brand creative team
- Build and maintain weekly, monthly, and quarterly performance reports and attribution dashboards using Google Analytics 4, Northbeam, or Triple Whale
- Partner with the lifecycle marketing team on audience strategy: lookalikes, retargeting, exclusions, and email-to-paid coordination
- Develop and present go-to-market paid media plans for new product launches and seasonal campaigns to VP and CMO-level stakeholders
- Identify emerging channels and test-and-learn opportunities, including Connected TV, Reddit, and retail media networks
- Manage one direct report (Performance Marketing Specialist) with potential to build a larger team over 12 months

Required Qualifications
- 4 or more years of performance marketing experience with direct hands-on management of paid social and paid search campaigns
- Demonstrated experience managing monthly budgets of 500,000 dollars or more across Meta and Google
- Proficiency in Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, and TikTok Ads Manager — hands-on, not just oversight
- Strong analytical skills: comfortable building reports, interpreting attribution data, and communicating performance to non-marketing stakeholders
- Experience briefing and evaluating creative for direct response campaigns, including UGC and short-form video
- Proven track record of scaling ROAS or improving CAC efficiency at a DTC or e-commerce brand
- Excellent written and verbal communication — able to produce executive-level reports and lead client or stakeholder presentations

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience marketing a hardware or wearable consumer product
- Familiarity with multi-touch attribution tools: Northbeam, Triple Whale, Rockerbox, or similar
- Experience managing and developing direct reports
- International market expansion experience (EU, APAC, or LATAM)
- Experience with influencer and creator partnership programmes as a paid amplification channel

Compensation and Benefits

- Equity grant with 4-year vesting schedule
- Employer-paid health, dental, and vision
- 2,000 dollar annual wellness stipend (product credits included)
